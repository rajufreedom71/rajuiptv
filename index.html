Setup Guide

1

Download an IPTV Player

Install any IPTV player on your device. We recommend IPTV Smarters Pro for mobile/TV or VLC Media Player for PC.

2

Copy the Free M3U Playlist URL

Copy the open-source streaming link provided below:

https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rajufreedom71/rajuiptv/main/playlist.m3u
3

Load the Link & Enjoy

Open your player application, navigate to "Add M3U URL" or "Open Network Stream", paste the copied URL, and enjoy endless channels instantly.
